How I got started

After 8 years working as a personal trainer, I felt it was a time for a change. During my time in the fitness industry i was self employed and at one stage worked with a web developer to help market my services. It was here that i realized the power and utility of having an online presence. Ultimately this is something i wanted to understand more about and help create platforms for other professionals.

Before beginning my personal trainer career I had just completed my degree in theoretical physics which involved some coding allowing me to become familiar with the basics of computing. So I embarked upon some self study and eventually enrolled with the Code Institute’s full stack developer course. I have projects written in a variety of popular front end and back end languages (detailed below).
